## Project Summary

### Vision
The first community in LATAM where gaming is the meeting point to level up in physical, mental, and social health — and in real life. "Gamers que se mueven, se cuidan y se encuentran."

### Problem
Three documented health issues among adult gamers, unaddressed in all of LATAM (355M gamers):
1. **Sedentarism/obesity**: 67% of Ecuadorian adults overweight/obese, gamers sit 12+ hrs/day
2. **Mental health**: 37% of competitive players show clinical depression/anxiety symptoms
3. **Social isolation**: Digitally connected but physically alone — online community ≠ real contact

### Three Operating Layers
| Layer | Name | Function |
|-------|------|----------|
| 1 | Media | Digital platforms (web, social, app) that educate and convene gamers on wellness |
| 2 | Social Hub | Physical + digital spaces (Discord + events) for real community |
| 3 | Audience Activator | Connects health/insurance/wellness companies with qualified gamer audience — B2B revenue engine |

### Business Model
- **Gamer never pays** — free community participation
- Companies pay to access a qualified audience with behavioral/health/habit data (generated with explicit user consent)
- Revenue: B2B data sales to insurers, hospitals, brands + sponsorships + premium memberships + content

### Events Architecture (3 Levels)
1. **Meetups** (free frequency): Themed by game — Caminata Dota, Run Free Fire, Senderismo RPG, Chill Zone Minecraft, Foodie Gamer
2. **General Event** (monthly/bimonthly): Convergence of all clusters — brand monetization, panels, tournaments, activations
3. **Clubs** (permanent): Meetups that reach critical mass become stable clubs with own identity and leadership

### Digital Stack
Discord (hub) → YouTube (authority content) → TikTok (discovery) → Instagram (visual brand) → WhatsApp (LATAM comms) → Web/App (data platform) → Twitch (live streaming)

### Data Model (Big Data)
Collects: demographics, gaming habits, event behavior, self-reported health, digital engagement, brand interaction
Monetized to: insurers (risk profiles), hospitals (preventive health), food brands (consumption profiles), fitness brands (behavior change), financial sector (spending profiles)

### Phases
1. **Foundations (0-3 months)**: Name, Discord, social media, first 3 meetups in Quito/Guayaquil, activate initial alliances
2. **Community (3-9 months)**: Monthly General Event, stable clubs, YouTube launch, web/app with data consent, gaming brand alliances
3. **Monetization & Scale (9-24 months)**: B2B data model active, expand to Colombia/Mexico/Peru, premium memberships, insurance gamification
